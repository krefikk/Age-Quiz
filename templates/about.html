{% if session.get('lang', 'tr') == 'en' %} Welcome to Age Quiz! This application is a fun and interactive game where you test your ability to guess ages based on images. Enjoy playing and sharing your results!
There are two game modes, Quiz and Marathon. In Quiz mode, you guess 20 random questions. In Marathon mode, you get 3 lives and you can keep guessing until you run out of them. But beware, time is also important in marathon mode.

All data and images used in the application were obtained from UTKFace's dataset (Face Dataset) and no additional sources were used. If you believe that any of these images violate your rights, you can have the content removed from the dataset by contacting one of the relevant persons indicated on UTKFace's website.

This application is available for non-commercial purposes only.
